# Email Templates for HRIS System

This directory contains all email notification templates for the HRIS system.

## Available Templates

1. **leave-approved.blade.php** - Sent to employees when their leave application is approved
2. **leave-rejected.blade.php** - Sent to employees when their leave application is rejected
3. **leave-cancelled.blade.php** - Sent to employees when they cancel their leave application
4. **leave-submitted.blade.php** - Sent to HR team when an employee submits a leave application
5. **user-approved.blade.php** - Sent to users when their account registration is approved
6. **user-registered.blade.php** - Sent to HR team when a new user registers
7. **training-assigned.blade.php** - Sent to employees when a training is assigned to them
8. **training-completed.blade.php** - Sent to employees when they complete a training
9. **holiday-added.blade.php** - Sent to all users when a new holiday is added
10. **holiday-updated.blade.php** - Sent to all users when a holiday is updated
11. **notice-created.blade.php** - Sent to all users when a new notice is published
12. **notice-updated.blade.php** - Sent to all users when a notice is updated
13. **notice-deleted.blade.php** - Sent to all users when a notice is removed

## Template Features

- Clean, responsive HTML layout
- Professional styling with CSS
- Consistent branding across all emails
- Proper header and footer sections
- Highlighted sections for important information
- Responsive design for various devices
